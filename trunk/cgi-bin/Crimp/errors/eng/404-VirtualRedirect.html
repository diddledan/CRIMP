404 - Page Not Found

A NON - FATAL ERROR has occured within the VirtualRedirect plugin module.
Please check the output of Debug View by appending ?debug=on to the URL.

Possible Reasons and Solutions

  1. The URL could not be found.
    • Check the URL can be accessed from your browser and it is spelt correctly in your crimp.ini file.

  2. The LWP::UserAgent fails to connect.
    • Check that your webhost actually allows the use of this perl module.
    • Check if your webhost requires that you need to use a proxy and set DefaultProxy in the global section of your crimp.ini file.
