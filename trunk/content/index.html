Welcome to TOTAL control of your website...

Introduction

If you have ever wanted to use multiple web applications for one site, then CRIMP is for you. Total configuration at your fingertips!

Create all your web content from ANY source using 1 simple config file... Merge together your blog, forum and even content from ANYWHERE and put them into a template of your own creation...

Still limited? Well, you can create what you want from where you want... use a single page template for your entire site or even have a different template for different directories.

How it works

The CRIMP engine uses a simple config file that calls certain modules to create content from any thing and any where.

Modules

Current CRIMP modules allow you to insert local content or other websites into a template of your design.
Future modules could enable RSS feeds, samples of live current IRC conversations and just about anything you can think of, to be inserted into your site on the fly.
